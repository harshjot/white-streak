About Us

Get to know a little about us

InDeft Technology Solutions is a leading Solution Provider in Information Technology space especially on IT-Infrastructure, Application development and Automation. Our experienced approach, focus on digital IT, global expertise of solution design, Integrity, project management, technical expertise, intellectual property, partnerships and ability to do orchestration and automations have positioned us an emerging IT provider.

Founded in the year 2016 by two entrepreneurs, currently headquartered in Mumbai and an operational office in Pune.

Professional services capability sits at the core of our market offer, with a complementary portfolio of services, support and technology solutions based around Industry Best Practices. Our customers trust not just in our ability to build solutions, but also in the way we design, implement and support these solutions that makes their businesses run more efficiently, with lower TCO and higher productivity.

Our exceptionally skilled, committed and talented team of consultants, engineers, project managers and support teams integrate a wide range of technical, business and financial factors when designing the solution for your business.

Our engineering teams have hands-on experience in all aspects of IT solutions, deployment, operational management and maintenance support. Our world class architects design solutions that fit best within your environment and help achieve your business goals.


Our Vision:

To be a trusted, global IT Solution and Services Partner for our esteemed customers and OEMs, committed to achieve client's business objectives by delivering professional services & adopting innovative technologies.


Our Mission:

To be the Partner of Choice to the growing needs of customers by providing innovative business solutions, with our core competency of designing , consultancy in digital transformation and Automation, implementing hardware/software solutions, providing Infrastructure optimization services and product development.


Our Values:

  • Customer first
  • Quality is ultimate
  • Strive towards excellence
  • Believe in innovation

With its inception in 2016 InDeft Technology Solutions became one of the leading IT solution provider India and abroad. With our professional approach & experienced resources we have become a trusted name global IT Solution and Services Partner for our clients.
